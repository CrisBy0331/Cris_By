Xi'an Guidebook

Welcome to the Xi'an Guidebook! This guide provides essential information about the city, including its history, culture, and must-visit attractions.

History

Xi'an, one of the oldest cities in China, was the starting point of the Silk Road and served as the capital for several dynasties. Its rich history is reflected in its ancient architecture and cultural heritage. The city is home to the famous Terracotta Army, a UNESCO World Heritage site, which was discovered in 1974 and dates back to the Qin Dynasty.

Culture

Xi'an is renowned for its vibrant culture, which includes traditional music, dance, and cuisine. The city hosts various festivals throughout the year, celebrating its rich heritage and modern achievements. Don't miss trying local delicacies like Biangbiang noodles and Roujiamo.

Must-Visit Attractions

Key attractions include the ancient City Wall, Big Wild Goose Pagoda, and the Muslim Quarter. Each site offers a glimpse into the city's past and present, making them essential stops for any visitor.
